Death hot sauce recipe!

Some sauce not with the pepper we need!

The sauce that you need but don't deserve!
Use some superhot peppers!
Unless you are a pro chilehead chile eater, any superhot type will do!

Ingredients for 10-15 pizza

  • enough superhot peppers
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. prepare some brine
  2. cut peppers
  3. ferment cut peppers in a jar burping daily for 2-4 weeks
  4. blend all
  5. store in fridge, it will last 1 year
  6. enjoy the heat!
