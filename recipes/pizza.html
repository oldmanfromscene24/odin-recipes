Pizza recipe

A delicious neapolitan pizza!

The recipe for the most noble type of pizza: the neapolitan!
Use the best ingredients you can find!
And beware the temperature! It's one of the most important things!

Ingredients for 10-15 pizza

  • water: 1 liter
  • salt_ 40-60 grams
  • yeast: 0,1-3 grams
  • flour: 1,6-1,8 kg
  • tomato puree
  • mozzarella
  • basil leaves

Steps

  1. mix flour, water, salt and yeast
  2. rise for 8-24 hours
  3. divide in "panetti"
  4. roll out
  5. top with tomato puree and mozzarella
  6. cook at 430-480 CÂ° for 60-90 seconds
  7. top with basil leaves
