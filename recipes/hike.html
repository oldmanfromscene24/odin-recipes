Fail an hike recipe!

How to be sure to fail an hike!

The recipe for a sure hiking disaster!
It works best near winter solstice, when there's not much daylight!
It works even better it is on a wild place and if you are with friends to share the pain!

Ingredients

  • A mountain
  • Some hiking trail
  • As much D+ and D- as possible
  • No maps
  • No gps
  • No previous study
  • (optional) a couple of friends

Steps

  1. Start the hike late, since "it's short"
  2. Park far away, at least 1 hour of walk, from the starting point, since the road to the parking is too ruined
  3. Aim for a mountain and climb it
  4. At the top, aim for another mountain and climb it, since "there's still time"
  5. Go to the parking with complete darkness, since "it has only a way out that goes to the car"
  6. Realize that the last car on the parking goes away from another exit, completely opposite to the other, that you hadn't seen, since it' dark
  7. Enjoy choosing where to go and find your car!
